# CRM & Sales Management | Vinamra Jain™

## Project Background
The **CRM & Sales Management** platform is an enterprise-grade sales intelligence and performance optimization suite designed by **Vinamra Jain™**. It serves as a centralized hub for sales, marketing, product, and executive teams to collaborate, track performance, and drive strategic growth through data-driven insights.

## Core Vision
The mission of this project is to bridge the gap between field intelligence and executive decision-making. By providing specialized interfaces for different organizational roles, the platform ensures that every stakeholder has the exact tools they need to manage the sales lifecycle—from lead generation to contract renewal.

## Persona-Based Workspaces

### 1. Sales Representative Hub
*   **Focus**: Pipeline management and activity tracking.
*   **Key Features**: Personal quota attainment, lead follow-up, interaction logging, and opportunity creation.
*   **Data Usage**: Real-time access to the `OPPORTUNITIES`, `CONTACTS`, and `SALES_ACTIVITIES` datasets.

### 2. Sales Manager Hub
*   **Focus**: Team governance and deal orchestration.
*   **Key Features**: Team performance leaderboards, territory rebalancing, and a real-time **Discount Approval Inbox** for margin preservation.
*   **Data Usage**: Monitors `SALES_REPS` performance and `DISCOUNT_REQUESTS`.

### 3. Account Manager Hub
*   **Focus**: Retention and strategic account growth.
*   **Key Features**: Customer health tracking (NPS), strategic account planning, and automated renewal orchestration.
*   **Data Usage**: Leverages `ACCOUNT_PLANS`, `RENEWALS`, and `CUSTOMERS` satisfaction metrics.

### 4. Marketing Intelligence Hub
*   **Focus**: Demand generation and ROI optimization.
*   **Key Features**: Multi-channel campaign performance tracking, lead quality scoring, and seamless lead distribution to sales teams.
*   **Data Usage**: Analyzes `CAMPAIGNS` and `LEADS` data lake.

### 5. Product Bridge
*   **Focus**: Closing the feedback loop between sales and engineering.
*   **Key Features**: "Voice of the Customer" ledger, technical documentation sync, and feature request prioritization based on business impact.
*   **Data Usage**: Manages `FEEDBACK` and `FEATURE_REQUESTS`.

### 6. Executive Leadership Hub
*   **Focus**: Global business performance and revenue forecasting.
*   **Key Features**: Regional KPI snapshots, predictive revenue modeling, and integrated **Power BI Enterprise Analytics**.
*   **Data Usage**: Aggregates `EXECUTIVE_KPI_DATA`.

## Technical Architecture
*   **Framework**: Next.js 15 (App Router) for high-performance server/client rendering.
*   **UI/UX**: Tailored with Tailwind CSS and ShadCN UI, featuring a high-density enterprise aesthetic and dark-mode support.
*   **Backend**: Powered by **Firebase Firestore** for real-time CRUD operations and **Firebase Authentication** for secure access.
*   **Analytics**: Integrated **Power BI** iframes for deep-dive interactive BI reporting.
